## DESIGN SYSTEM NOTES

### Consistent Visual Language
- **Background color:** Light pastel blue (#D6EAF8 approx.) for all domestic/happy scenes
- **Dark blue/grey (#2C3E6B)** for emotional/lonely/crowd scenes
- **Warm golden (#F4D03F)** for summer/heat scenes
- **Night palette:** Dark grey with candlelight warm tones for intimate scenes
- **Aurora palette:** Greens, purples, pinks for "magical" climax moments

### Typography
- **On-screen text:** Rounded, bold, white with dark outline — always top-third of frame
- **Watermark:** "@pudgypenguins" in translucent grey, bottom-center

### Animation Principles
- **Squash & stretch:** Heavy on eating animations and emotional reactions
- **Anticipation:** Clear wind-up before actions (reaching for food, leaning in)
- **Follow-through:** Hair tuft and flipper tips trail slightly after movement
- **Staging:** Characters always centered or rule-of-thirds; camera follows the food
