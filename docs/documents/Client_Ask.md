# Pudgy Penguins — What We Need From You (Quick Version)

To train a custom Pax & Polly animation model, here's the short list. Full detail is in the longer data-request doc — this is the send-ready summary.

**Three rules that matter most**
1. Send **clean base renders** — your *no-watermark, no-text, no-timecode* exports (not the posted/social versions).
2. Where you can, give us the **character on a transparent background** and the **background/props separately** (so we control how they're combined).
3. Keep everything at **native 24 fps** and your **highest resolution** (≥1080 short edge).

---

## A. From your existing library
- [ ] **15–25 finished skits** as clean base renders (ProRes / PNG sequence). Pick for *variety of actions*, not just favorites.
- [ ] **Character-isolated passes** (Pax/Polly on transparent bg) + **clean background plates**, per skit — *or*, if easier, the layered exports / a "Collect Files" project zip.
- [ ] **Artifacts library** — recurring props (igloo, couch, table, chess set, iFin, bus, backpack, orca…) as **individual transparent PNGs**, plus each **background** as a clean plate. Keep your `Prp_*` names.
- [ ] **Pax & Polly model sheets** — turnarounds (multi-angle) + expression sheets, transparent PNG, with exact color codes.
- [ ] **Style guide access** — a Figma export of the model-sheet pages + Notion storyboard access (both are login-gated for us right now).
- [ ] **Shot list** for the skits you send — timecode, who's in it, the action, the background (a simple sheet; your existing tags are fine).

## B. New clips to shoot *for the model* (this is the high-value part)
Short, clean references — **one action, static camera, neutral gray background, 3–5 seconds.** Shot for training, not for social.
- [ ] **Single-action clips** covering each motion: walk, idle/breathe, blink, wave, head-turn, jump, sit, eat (aim ~5 each).
- [ ] **Turnaround videos** — a slow 360° rotation of Pax, Polly, and a few key props on neutral gray.
- [ ] **Two-character primitives** — Pax & Polly facing each other, lean-in, head-on-shoulder, flipper-hold, on neutral gray.
- [ ] **Loop-ready cycles** — idle / walk / bounce that start and end on the same pose.
- [ ] **Expression & eye-direction transitions** — neutral→happy, neutral→surprised, look left→right, blinks.

## C. Two quick answers (✓ confirmed)
- [x] Frame rate — **true 24 fps** (not 12 doubled).
- [x] Prop animation — **any prop can animate** depending on the skit/prompt (props aren't inherently static).

---
**Not needed yet:** audio (first outputs are silent looping GIFs). **Delivery:** any shared drive/link, a folder per item. If any item is hard to produce, tell us — we have fallbacks.
